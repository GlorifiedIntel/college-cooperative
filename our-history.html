Our Story

The Origin

The College of Education, Akwanga Cooperative, Thrift and Credit Society, Limited was established on 17th December, 1997 by The Provost Chief, Rev., Dr. Musa Bawa (Late) during an enlightenment meeting with the College Staff. During the meeting, the Provost explained the importance of Cooperative Society to the College Community.

Aims & Objectives

  1. To promote thrift among members, offering them the opportunity to save, and mange thier finances well.
  2. To enable members obtain loan at low rates (10%, 15%, 25% and 35%) with a repayment plan the is friendly.
  3. To invest in any business that members endorse which will be profitable to the cooperative
  4. To buy and sell to members personal items with little inteerest as deemed necessary by the management or at members' request.
  5. To buy and sell to members and non-members landed properties, and buildings where the need arise
  6. To provide recreational facilities in the College for members and Guest, and to provide the necessary resources for the welfare of all members especially in areas of education
  7. To buy developmental stocks, treasury bills, and security guaranteed by the government time-to-time, and to encourage members to save
