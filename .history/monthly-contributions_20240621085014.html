Monthly Contributions

Monthly Contribution Regular Loan Emergency Loan Car Loan Financial Planning Financial Consult

Cooperative monthly contributions are a way for members to pool their money together on a regular basis. Members have diferent reasons or purpose for contributing monthly. Some contribute to buy land, some to build a house, some to save, some for reasons best known to them. We encourage members to contribute monthly. Your monthly contributions determine how much you can borrow and the interest rate you will pay. The cooperative share the profits with members based on their contributions.

The Cooperative have two types of contributions or savings:

  • Regular Savings
  • Special Deposit

Note:

  • Minimum Regular Savings for Junior Staff is ₦2000
  • Minimum Regular Savings for Senior Staff is ₦5000
  • You determine the amount you want to be depositing every month as your special Deposit.
