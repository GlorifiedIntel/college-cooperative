We salute you!

The College of Education, Akwanga Staff Cooperative and Thrift & Credit Society is more than just a financial institution; it's a community. We are a pillar of support for our college staff, fostering financial stability and empowering them to achieve their goals. We look forward to continuing to serve our members for generations to come.

Our History

Our Roots: A Legacy of Support

Today, the Society remains dedicated to its core mission: promoting financial literacy, encouraging savings habits, and providing accessible and affordable credit solutions to our members. We offer a variety of services, including:

  • Savings Accounts : Secure saving options with competitive interest rates.
  • Loans : Flexible loan products to meet your personal and professional needs, from educational pursuits to home improvement.
  • Financial Workshops : Educational programs to empower members to make informed financial decisions.

Our Future: Building a Brighter Tomorrow

The Society is constantly evolving to meet the changing needs of our members. We are committed to:

  • Technological Advancement : Investing in user-friendly online platforms to provide convenient access to services.
  • Expanding Services : Exploring new financial products and services that cater to the diverse needs of our college staff.
  • Financial Education : Continuing to offer financial literacy workshops to promote informed financial planning.
